Ice Cream

Description

Ice cream is a popular frozen dessert made from dairy products such as milk and cream, combined with sweeteners and flavorings.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups heavy cream
  • 1 cup whole milk
  • 3/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 tablespoon pure vanilla extract
  • A pinch of salt
  • Mixing bowls
  • Whisk
  • Freezer-safe container

Steps

  1. Combine Ingredients
  2. Chill the Mixture
  3. Churn the Ice Cream
  4. Freeze the Ice Cream
  5. Serve
