Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a traditional Italian dish made by layering sheets of pasta with various ingredients such as cheese, meat, vegetables, and sauce.

Ingredients

  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 1 pound ground beef or Italian sausage
  • 1 jar (24 ounces) marinara sauce
  • 2 cups ricotta cheese
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 egg
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Optional: 1 cup chopped onions, 2 cloves minced garlic, 2 cups fresh spinach or other vegetables, 1 teaspoon dried basil, 1 teaspoon dried oregano

Steps

  1. Preheat the Oven to 375 degrees Fahrenheit (190 degrees Celsius)
  2. Cook the Noodles
  3. Prepare the Meat Sauce
  4. Mix the Ricotta Filling
  5. Assemble the Lasagna
  6. Bake the Lasagna
  7. Let It Rest
