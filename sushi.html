Sushi

Description

Sushi is a traditional Japanese dish consisting of vinegared rice, often accompanied by various ingredients such as raw or cooked seafood, vegetables, and sometimes tropical fruits.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups sushi rice (short-grain rice)
  • 2 cups water
  • 1/4 cup rice vinegar
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • Nori (seaweed sheets)
  • Fillings (e.g., cucumber, avocado, crab sticks, smoked salmon, tuna, etc.)
  • Soy sauce
  • Pickled ginger
  • Wasabi

Steps

  1. Prepare the Sushi Rice
  2. Cook the Noodles
  3. Prepare the Fillings
  4. Set Up Your Rolling Station
  5. Roll the Sushi
  6. Slice the Sushi Rolls
  7. Yummy!
